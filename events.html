Workshop on Machine Learning

Date: 29/10/2024 & 30/10/2024

Description:IEEE ComSoc SBC NSSCE and IEEE EdSoc SBC NSSCE, in collaboration with CNC Lab, hosted a two-day Machine Learning Workshop on October 29-30, from 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM at CNC Lab. Designed for beginners, the workshop provided an engaging introduction to machine learning concepts with expert insights and hands-on exercises. Dr. Sruthy Manmadhan led the first session, covering supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning, with a focus on supervised learning, data collection, model training, and validation. Participants gained practical experience working with sample datasets. On the second day, Dr. Anuraj Mohan explored classifiers, logistic regression, and neural networks, providing a strong foundation in ML model operations and real-world applications. The event concluded with Dr. Vijitha S presenting a token of appreciation to the speakers. The workshop successfully equipped attendees with foundational ML knowledge, preparing them for advanced applications.

6G Unleashed

Date: 30/07/2024

Description: On July 30, 2024, IEEE ComSoc SBC NSSCE hosted an online session, "6G Unleashed: The Future of Wireless Communication and Beyond," led by Dr. Ayappasamy, Research Engineer and IEEE Member from Digital University Kerala. The session explored the evolution of wireless communication from 2G to 6G, highlighting 5G limitations and advancements like Visible Light Communication (VLC), Intelligent Reflecting Surfaces (IRS), ultra-massive MIMO, semantic communication, and Terahertz (THz) technology.
