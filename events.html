Introduction to LabVIEW

Introduction to LabVIEW

The session introduced students to LabVIEW, a graphical programming platform widely used in automation, data acquisition, and hardware interfacing. Athul Prasad explained its core components, including the Front Panel, Block Diagram, and Virtual Instruments (VIs), with a hands-on demonstration where participants created a basic Virtual Instrument. The event highlighted LabVIEW‚Äôs real-world applications in automation, robotics, and scientific research, emphasizing its role in data acquisition and process control. The interactive session received positive feedback, encouraging students to explore LabVIEW for academic projects and career opportunities. This successful collaboration provided a valuable platform for knowledge sharing and skill development in graphical programming and automation technologies.

WORKSHOP ON MACHINE LEARNING

Date: 29/10/2024 & 30/10/24

Description: IEEE ComSoc SBC NSSCE and IEEE EdSoc SBC NSSCE, in collaboration with CNC Lab, hosted a two-day Machine Learning Workshop on October 29 -30, from 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM at CNC Lab. Designed for beginners, the workshop provided an engaging introduction to machine learning concepts with expert insights and hands-on exercises. Dr. Sruthy Manmadhan led the first session, covering supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning, with a focus on supervised learning, data collection, model training, and validation. Participants gained practical experience working with sample datasets. On the second day, Dr. Anuraj Mohan explored classifiers, logistic regression, and neural networks, providing a strong foundation in ML model operations and real-world applications. The event concluded with Dr. Vijitha S presenting a token of appreciation to the speakers. The workshop successfully equipped attendees with foundational ML knowledge, preparing them for advanced applications.

6G Unleashed

Date: 30/07/2024

SPECTRUM: Unveiling the Power of Radio Communication

Date: March 29, 2024 | Time: 7 PM | Platform: Online

Introduction to Radio Communication

Dr. Ghandhiraj R introduces the session on radio communication fundamentals.

Practical Demonstrations

Participants engage with real-time tools like WebSDR and Flight Radar.

Description: IEEE ComSoc SBC NSSCE conducted an online session, "SPECTRUM: Unveiling the Power of Radio Communication," on March 29, 2024, at 7 PM, led by Dr. Ghandhiraj R, Associate Professor, Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Coimbatore. The session covered radio communication fundamentals, real-time tools like WebSDR and Flight Radar, and software such as GNU Radio for signal processing. Participants gained valuable insights into modern radio technologies and resources for further learning, making it an engaging and informative session.
